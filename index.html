01 — Who We Are

We fund what changes the odds.

Read Our Mandate

The Zuko Tisani Youth Leadership Foundation removes the financial barriers that stop capable young South Africans from starting, competing, working, and representing their country.

We fund specific, once-off gateways into opportunity — registrations, small business assets, sporting kit, travel to events, and rare special-case aid — paid directly to the institution or supplier.
